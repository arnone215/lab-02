If the weather is too rough, I can still stay occupied by catching up on some of my favorite TV shows or playing some video games on Xbox Live. Regardless of what weather the PNW throws at us, I am always on a mission to try new places to eat or have a couple drinks. I have spent some time cooking in kitchens and have always been a foodie!
